About Me

Hi, my name is Akornefa Akyea and I am a full stack developer based in Madison, WI. Before entering the world of web development, I worked in the non profit world as social media director and content marketer for Brooklyn-based organizations. My curiousity about the HTML in the Mailchimp newsletters I was creating as a media director led me to take a coding course, and that quickly turned into a full stack bootcamp!

I have a B.A. in Music from Columbia Univerisity. When I am not working I love practicing the flute, piano, guitar, bass guitar and writing songs. I am constantly creating musically and I am eager to bring this same creativity and enthusiasm to your web and mobile based projects.
